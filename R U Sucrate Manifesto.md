### R U Sucrate Manifesto

R U Sucrate is an endless source of inspiration for creators;
I hope that when I create, I can devote 100% of my experience to the creation itself, without worrying about anything else;
I hope RUS can incubate ideas;
I hope RUS understands exactly what I want;
I hope when my nerves need to relax, I can hear music that is just right;

I hope that when I drink coffee, my mood is not 'I just did really well,' but 'I didn't expect I could do it this way.'
